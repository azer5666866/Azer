It is the most popular sport in the world and is played on a rectangular field with a goal at each end. Players primarily use their feet to control and pass the ball, but they can also use their head and chest. Only the goalkeeper is allowed to use their hands, and only within their penalty area. A standard match is played in two halves of 45 minutes each. Football is known for its simple rules and global appeal, making it a favorite pastime for millions of fans and players around the world.
